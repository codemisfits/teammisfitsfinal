what happens if i declare late?

P20 000 penalty for failing to declare on time. A daily penalty of P200 if a declaration is not submitted within 30 days of the person being issued with a notice directing him or her to declare. In case of a member of Parliament, they are not to sit in the National Assembly, until such time as the declaration as submitted. In the case of a former president, suspension of pension or benefits for the period that no declaration is made. In the case of any other person, disciplinary action under the relevant Act is taken, including removal from office or dismissal.
